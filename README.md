# Furniture Sales Insights
This project analyzes **2 years of furniture sales and customer data** to uncover trends in purchases, top-selling products, and customer behavior. The goal is to provide **actionable insights** for marketing and product strategy.

## Data Used
- **Orders**: Details of customer purchases
- **Products**: Furniture product catalog and pricing
- **Customers**: Customer information and demographics
- **Ratings/Reviews**: Customer feedback and product ratings

## Analysis Performed
- **Sales Analysis**: Revenue per product, units sold, top-selling products
- **Customer Analysis**: Top customers by revenue, purchase patterns
- **RFM Segmentation**: Recency, Frequency, Monetary analysis to identify valuable customer segments
- **Review Analysis**: Top-reviewed products and average ratings
- **Visualizations**: Bar charts, gradients, and trend analyses to communicate insights clearly

## Key Insights
- Identified **top-selling furniture items** and products generating the highest revenue
- Highlighted **most valuable customers** using RFM segmentation
- Provided actionable recommendations for **marketing campaigns** and **product strategy**
- Visualized **customer engagement and product performance** for better business decisions

## Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- Matplotlib & Seaborn for visualizations
- Jupyter Notebook

## How to Use
1. Clone the repository
2. Open `Furniture_Sales_Insights.ipynb` in Jupyter Notebook
3. Run cells sequentially to explore data, visualizations, and insights

## Outcome
This project demonstrates the ability to **analyze sales data, generate business insights, and communicate findings** effectively, which are key skills for a Business Analyst role.
